Great Music, Memorable Performances

Core Memory Music is a performing arts venture in southern Rhode Island. We present chamber music concerts in a modern home overlooking a scenic mill pond. Accomplished musicians from around the world come here to perform great music in a relaxed, informal setting.

The address of the venue is 103 North Road, Wakefield, Rhode Island 02879. Driving time from Providence, Newport, Westerly, and southeastern Connecticut is under an hour, and Amtrak’s Kingston Station is only five miles away.
